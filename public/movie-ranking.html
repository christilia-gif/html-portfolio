The best movies of all time (according to Christilia)

These are my top 3 movies! Based on my taste and maybe a little of nostalgia.

The suitcase

This is an amazing movie. Has pleanty of action and plot twists and will leave you hangin!

The Truman show

No words needed for this masterpiece. It is trully a reality check and will leave you questioning alternative realities.
Let alone Jim Carrey being one of the best actors of all time in my sencere opinion

The Purge

I have a thing for "almost real" sci fi scenarios. There is something dystopian about them. Again, talks about something that if happened, would be one of our worst nightmares.
To my opinion, aliens invasion is more light-hearted than what happens in the purge.
